I'm a website developer & designer, self-taught in HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, Tailwind, JavaScript, TypeScript, PHP, C# & Angular, with a BA (Hons) in Business Studies.

I started my career in digtal advertising and after a short time moved into advertising recruitment. I eventually set up & began running my own award nominated media recruitment business, alongside a job board for the media industry. After all of this I moved into in-house talent acquistion for major, international media companies, content marketing agencies & digital design agencies such as News UK, Dare & The River Group.
